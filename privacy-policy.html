Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the mobile application "Hint & Guess - Taboo Party Game" (the "App").

1. WHO WE ARE

This App is a party word-guessing game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about data practices.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personal identification.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • The App uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. When ads are displayed, Google may collect certain data (such as device identifiers, advertising ID, and general usage data) in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not control this data collection. You can learn more at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • The App uses Internet and network state only to load ads and does not send your game content or scores to our servers.

2.3 Data stored on your device

  • Game preferences (e.g. language, time per round, number of teams) and in-session data (e.g. scores during a game) may be stored locally on your device.
  • This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

  • We do not use any personal data for our own purposes.
  • Ad serving is handled by Google AdMob. Google may use collected data to show relevant ads and for analytics as described in their policies.

4. DATA SHARING

  • We do not sell or share your personal data.
  • Third-party ad networks (including Google AdMob) may collect and use data as described in their privacy policies.
  • We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. DATA SECURITY

We do not store your personal data on our servers. Any local data on your device is subject to your device's security and your control (e.g. uninstalling the App removes local app data).

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## ğŸ² How to Play

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Choose a Word**
   - One player picks a word (without telling others)
   - Word should be appropriate for all players

2. **Give Hints**
   - Give creative hints without saying the word directly
   - Use descriptions, analogies, or related words
   - Make it challenging but guessable

3. **Guess the Word**
   - Other players race to guess the word
   - Teams compete against each other
   - First correct guess wins the round!

4. **Score Points**
   - Fastest correct guess scores maximum points
   - Teams accumulate points across rounds
   - Higher score wins the game

## ğŸ¯ Game Rules

### Basic Rules:
- **No Direct Words**: You cannot say the word or any part of it
- **No Names**: Don't use proper nouns or brand names (unless allowed)
- **No Rhyming**: Don't rhyme with the word or give rhyming clues
- **No Sign Language**: Stick to verbal hints (depends on game settings)
- **Time Limit**: Each round has a time limit (customizable)

### Scoring:
- Correct guess: 10 points
- Speed bonus: 5 points (if guessed quickly)
- Team completion: 15 points

### Winning:
- First team to reach target score wins
- Highest score after all rounds wins
- Customizable win conditions
